About Our Organization

Rotary Club of Udaipur Udyam comprises dedicated business and professional leaders using entrepreneurial strategy to build permanent humanitarian infrastructure.

Humanitarian Philosophy

People of Action Serving Communities

Rotary's primary mission is to promote understanding, fellowship, and peace globally. At Udaipur Udyam, we execute this mandate locally by identifying marginalized forest zones in Aravalli hills and applying robust utility solutions.

We do not operate through short-term handouts. We build community training grids, install long-lasting polycrystalline solar units, and support local students so families can live safely and cleanly.

Club Pillars of Conduct

  • Service Integrity: Maintaining active databases of every dollar received and hardware deployed.
  • Local Ownership: Establishing village maintenance groups to ensure solar devices are sustained long-term.
  • Environmental Focus: Replacing fossil-burning Kerosene fumes with zero-pollution energy.
Milestones

Our Timeline

A look at the key developmental phases of our organization in Udaipur.

2021

Organization Charter

Officially chartered under Rotary District 3054. Formed a board of local Udaipur entrepreneurs committed to structural community service.

2022

Grassroots Surveys & Pilot Programs

Initiated field audits of Kaya Village forest pockets. Distributed safety school benches and lanterns to local children studying in low-grid regions.

2023

Pathway Grids & Sacred Lighting

Constructed pilot pathway micro-grids and lit community gathering spaces, establishing hubs that function entirely off-grid.

2024

Flagship solar rollout

Officially launched the Akshay Oorja campaign to install rooftop solar modules, battery setups, and double LED tubes inside isolated forest blocks.

2025

Scaling Operations

Reached a milestone of 850+ solar installations, improving indoor air quality and study environments for 4,500+ rural residents.

2026

Smart Education & Expansion

Deploying solar-equipped digital centers, setting up recycling paths for lithium cells, and extending clean water wells access points.
