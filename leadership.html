President Message

Leading With Commitment

"True humanitarian support doesn't lie in distributing temporary materials. We operate like entrepreneurs: mapping village grids, auditing components durability, and organizing training sessions so local residents can maintain devices. The Akshay Oorja Campaign exemplifies this sustainable framework, lighting Aravalli homes left in darkness for a century."

Devendra Choudhary

President, Rotary Club of Udaipur Udyam
