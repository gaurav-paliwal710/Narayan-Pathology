What We Do

Our Focus Pillars

We target structural issues systematically across multiple sectors to maximize community resilience.

Empower Education

Supplying local tribal children with books, desks, and safety solar lanterns so they can study safely after sunset.

Renewable Energy

Installing robust roof solar setups in forest communities that are legally restricted from physical grid grids.

Save Environment

Protecting local ecosystems by transitioning off-grid villages from fire-hazard wood and kerosene energy.

Healthcare Support

Promoting clean indoor air quality by removing toxic smoke-emitting fossil lamps that trigger lung infections.

Infrastructure Aid

Installing lighting grids on high-risk public paths and safety nodes in core community gathering temples.

Community Development

Training regional youth in device maintenance and management systems to encourage local micro-economies.

Flagship Initiative

Akshay Oorja Campaign

The communities near Kaya Village-Vandra Phalla reside inside ancestral forest lands. Standard power utility lines are restricted because of forest preservation policies.

Our Akshay Oorja campaign distributes battery-backed solar packages, successfully illuminating households and school paths while remaining zero-emission.
